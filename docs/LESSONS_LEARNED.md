## 📈 Marketing & Community
- **Visuals > Text**: A technical CLI still needs a "Killer Screenshot." The 11.11% Drift comparison image is the strongest asset.
- **Reddit Showoff Saturday**: Highly competitive and strictly moderated. Post early (GMT morning) to catch both EU and US traffic.
- **Engagement Guide**: Having a "Skeptic Response Rule" (Rules 1-5) is vital for developer tooling.

## 💻 Tooling & Environment
- **Browser Automation (Edge)**: `browser_subagent` in Windows works best when Edge is prioritized and specific extensions/login states are leveraged.
- **CLI Call Protocol**: Bypassing problematic slash commands (`/plan`, `/fast`) in automated CLI runners saves time and prevents hang-up errors on Windows.
